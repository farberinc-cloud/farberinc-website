Frequently Asked Questions

What is Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)?

GEO is the practice of optimizing your brand, content, and entity presence to be cited by generative AI platforms — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude. It is the next evolution of SEO, focused on the AI-mediated discovery path that increasingly replaces direct search.

How is GEO different from SEO?

SEO optimizes for ranked links on a search results page. GEO optimizes for citations inside AI-generated answers. The tactics overlap (entity clarity, authoritative sources, structured data) but the success metrics are different — GEO measures citation frequency and answer presence, not just rankings.

What is the best GEO strategy for a B2B business in 2026?

The highest-leverage GEO strategy for B2B in 2026 is: (1) build a strong entity graph that AI engines can map, (2) get authoritative third-party coverage on sources AI engines retrieve, (3) publish source-of-truth content that directly answers category-defining questions, and (4) implement structured data that AI engines can parse.
