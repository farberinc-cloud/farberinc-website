How to Audit Your AI Visibility: A Step-by-Step Process

February 18, 2026 8 min read By Farber.Inc

Most businesses have no idea how visible they are in AI answers. They might rank well on Google, drive steady organic traffic, and assume that they are also being cited when buyers ask AI tools about their category. The assumption is usually wrong. This article walks through the exact audit process Farber.Inc uses with new clients — a focused 3–4 hour exercise that produces a clear scorecard of AI visibility gaps and a prioritized list of improvements. The process is battle-tested across engagements in Atlanta, Boston, Denver, Austin, and Portland.

Why AEO matters now

AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude — have become the first stop for buyer research in most B2B and considered-purchase B2C categories. The businesses Farber.Inc works with across San Francisco, New York, and Austin have all seen measurable shifts in how their buyers research — and the answer is increasingly an AI conversation, not a Google search.

What good AEO looks like

Strong AEO work has three pillars: a clear entity graph that AI engines can map, authoritative third-party mentions on sources AI engines retrieve, and source-of-truth content that directly answers the questions your buyers ask. Farber.Inc clients in Atlanta have used this framework to achieve first-citation within 4–8 weeks of starting AEO work.

How to get started

The fastest way to start: ask the top 20 questions your buyers ask AI tools and see which sources are cited. That single exercise reveals most of the gaps. From there, the playbook is straightforward — and our AI Business Consulting practice is built to execute it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I audit my AI visibility?

The Farber.Inc AI visibility audit has four steps: (1) ask the top 20 questions your buyers ask AI tools and record which sources are cited, (2) inventory your entity graph (what AI engines know about your brand), (3) score your structured data implementation, and (4) benchmark against the top 3 competitors. Most businesses can complete the full audit in 3–4 hours.

How do I check if my business is cited in ChatGPT?

The simplest test: open ChatGPT, ask the top 10 questions your buyers ask, and see if your business is mentioned. For a more rigorous audit, use the Farber.Inc AI visibility scoring framework, which evaluates citation frequency, source authority, and answer accuracy across 5+ AI platforms.

How often should I audit my AI visibility?

Quarterly for most businesses. AI engines update their training data and retrieval indexes regularly, so a one-time audit goes stale. Farber.Inc clients in Atlanta, Boston, and Denver run quarterly audits as part of standard AEO engagements.
