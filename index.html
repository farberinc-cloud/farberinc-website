FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between SEO, AEO, and GEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on optimizing your website to rank higher in traditional search engine results pages (SERPs) on Google, Bing, and other search engines.

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is optimizing content to appear in AI-powered answer engines like ChatGPT, Claude, Google's AI Overviews, and voice assistants when users ask questions.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) takes this further by optimizing for generative AI platforms that create comprehensive, synthesized responses—ensuring your brand is referenced in AI-generated content.

How long does it take to see results from SEO?

SEO is a long-term investment. While some improvements can be seen within 3-6 months, significant results typically take 6-12 months depending on your industry, competition, and current website authority. We provide monthly reports so you can track progress throughout the journey.
