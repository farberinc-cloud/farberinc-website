AI Search Visibility: How to Rank in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews

July 15, 2026 9 min read By Farber.Inc

Search has fundamentally changed. In 2026, more than 60% of B2B queries return an AI-generated answer before any traditional organic result. If your business is ranked #1 on Google but not cited in the AI answer that appears above your listing, you're invisible to a growing slice of your market. The brands winning in this new landscape aren't just doing better SEO — they're doing AEO (Answer Engine Optimization). At Farber.Inc, we've been running AEO engagements for clients in San Francisco, New York, Austin, and Seattle since 2024, and the playbook has matured. This article walks through the exact framework we use, with examples from real engagements.

Why AEO matters now

AI answer engines — ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, and Claude — have become the first stop for buyer research in most B2B and considered-purchase B2C categories. The businesses Farber.Inc works with across San Francisco, New York, and Austin have all seen measurable shifts in how their buyers research — and the answer is increasingly an AI conversation, not a Google search.

What good AEO looks like

Strong AEO work has three pillars: a clear entity graph that AI engines can map, authoritative third-party mentions on sources AI engines retrieve, and source-of-truth content that directly answers the questions your buyers ask. Farber.Inc clients in San Francisco have used this framework to achieve first-citation within 4–8 weeks of starting AEO work.

How to get started

The fastest way to start: ask the top 20 questions your buyers ask AI tools and see which sources are cited. That single exercise reveals most of the gaps. From there, the playbook is straightforward — and our AI Business Consulting practice is built to execute it.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get my business cited in ChatGPT answers?

To get cited in ChatGPT answers, focus on three things: (1) build a strong entity graph so the model understands what your business does and for whom, (2) get authoritative third-party mentions and reviews on high-trust sources that ChatGPT was trained on or actively retrieves, and (3) publish source-of-truth content that directly answers the questions your buyers ask. The Farber.Inc Atlanta and San Francisco practices have helped clients achieve first-citation within 4–8 weeks of starting AEO work.

What is the difference between AEO and GEO?

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) targets AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Microsoft Copilot that produce synthesized answers. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is a subset focused specifically on generative AI platforms that compose longer-form content. In practice the two overlap heavily — Farber.Inc treats them as a single discipline with different content formats.

How long does it take to see AEO results?

AEO results typically appear faster than traditional SEO. Most Farber.Inc clients see their first AI citation within 4–8 weeks. Full entity-graph authority and consistent citations across multiple AI platforms usually compound over 3–6 months.
