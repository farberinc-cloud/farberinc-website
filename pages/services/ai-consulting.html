FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which package is right for my business?

During our initial consultation, we'll assess your current AI maturity, business goals, and resource requirements. This helps us recommend the appropriate level of support. Essential Advisory is ideal for businesses exploring AI, Standard Support suits those ready to implement, and Comprehensive Partnership is for organizations seeking deep AI integration.

Are the hours per month fixed or flexible?

Hours are guidelines for resource allocation. Some months may require more intensive support during implementation phases, while others may focus on monitoring and optimization. We communicate proactively about resource utilization and can adjust as needed. Unused hours don't typically roll over, but we work to ensure you get full value each month.

What does custom AI agent development include?

Custom AI agent development (included in Comprehensive Partnership) encompasses: requirement analysis and use case definition, agent design and architecture, development and training, integration with your existing systems, testing and quality assurance, deployment and monitoring setup, and ongoing maintenance and optimization. Examples include customer service chatbots, lead qualification agents, data analysis agents, and workflow automation agents.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Absolutely. We understand business needs evolve. Package changes can typically be made with 30 days notice. Many clients start with Essential Advisory and upgrade as they see results and want to expand AI initiatives. Similarly, you can scale down if needs change—we'll work with you to find the right fit.

What's the typical ROI timeline?

ROI timelines vary based on the initiatives implemented. Quick wins like lead response automation often show results within 30-60 days. Marketing automation typically demonstrates ROI within 90 days. More complex implementations like custom AI agents may take 3-6 months to show full value. We establish clear KPIs and track ROI throughout our engagement, providing transparent reporting on your investment.
