FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to optimize a Google Business Profile?

Initial setup and optimization can be completed within 1-2 weeks. However, ongoing optimization and building review velocity is an ongoing process that typically shows significant results within 3-6 months. Verification of your profile can take anywhere from a few days to a few weeks depending on the verification method required.

What's the difference between Google Analytics Universal and GA4?

Google Analytics 4 (GA4) is the current generation of Google Analytics, replacing Universal Analytics (UA) which was sunsetted in July 2023. GA4 offers event-based tracking (vs. session-based), enhanced cross-platform tracking, AI-powered insights, better privacy controls, and more flexible reporting. If you're still using UA or haven't set up analytics, we strongly recommend implementing GA4.

How much should I budget for Google Ads?

Google Ads budgets vary significantly based on your industry, competition, and goals. We typically recommend starting with a minimum of $1,000-$3,000 per month for local campaigns and $5,000+ for competitive industries or national reach. During our consultation, we'll analyze your market and provide specific budget recommendations based on your target cost per acquisition and expected conversion rates.

Do I need all four Google platform services?

While each service provides unique value, we recommend at minimum having a properly optimized Google Business Profile and Google Analytics setup for any business. Google Search Console is essential for SEO, and Google Ads depends on your advertising goals and budget. During our initial consultation, we'll assess your needs and recommend the right combination of services for your business objectives.

Can you help recover from a Google penalty?

Yes, we have experience recovering websites from both manual actions and algorithmic penalties. The process involves identifying the root cause through a comprehensive audit, implementing necessary fixes, submitting reconsideration requests when applicable, and monitoring for recovery. Recovery timelines vary based on the severity and type of penalty.
