FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you post on social media?

Posting frequency varies by platform and your specific goals. Typically, we recommend 3-5 posts per week for Facebook and LinkedIn, daily posts for Instagram, and 1-3 times daily for TikTok. We'll develop a custom posting schedule based on your audience engagement patterns and platform best practices.

Do I need to provide content or do you create everything?

We handle all content creation as part of our comprehensive service. This includes graphics, copywriting, and video content. However, we love incorporating authentic behind-the-scenes content from our clients—your team, office, products, and customers—which we can professionally edit and optimize for each platform.

What platforms should my business be on?

The right platforms depend on your target audience and business type. B2B companies typically see best results on LinkedIn, while B2C brands often thrive on Instagram and Facebook. TikTok is excellent for reaching younger demographics. During our discovery process, we'll analyze your audience and recommend the optimal platform mix for your business.

How do you measure social media success?

We track multiple KPIs aligned with your goals: engagement rate, follower growth, reach and impressions, website traffic from social, lead generation, and conversions. We provide monthly reports with clear metrics and insights, along with strategic recommendations for continuous improvement.

What's included in your social media management packages?

Our comprehensive packages include content strategy development, content creation (graphics, copy, video), content calendar management, posting and scheduling, community management and engagement, monthly analytics reporting, and strategic optimization. Paid advertising management is available as an add-on service.
